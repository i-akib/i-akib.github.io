Research Topics

Merger Modelling of the Local Group Galaxies

Reproducing the observational parameters of the MW and the Andromeda galaxy (M31) by modeling their merger

Origin of VPOS Dwarf Galaxies

Investigating the origin of dwarf galaxies in the Vast Polar Structure (VPOS) of the Milky Way from the tidal tail ejected from the major merger at M31.

Timing Argument for the Local Group Mass

Investigating the timing argument calculation in the context of the major merger of the M31.

Dark Matter from the MW Rotation Curve

Analyzing the rotation curve of the Milky Way to deduce the distribution of the dark matter in the galaxy.

Orbits of the MW Dwarf Galaxies and Globular Clusters

Mapping and modeling the orbits of dwarf galaxies and globular clusters within the Milky Way to understand their origins and interactions.

Polariametric Study of Stars with Planets and Circumgalactic Disk

Calculating the polarisation of Stars in the Kepler field from their polarimetric observations.
