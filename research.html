Research Topics - Istiak Akib

Merger Modelling of Galaxies

Studying the complex gravitational interactions and dynamics during galaxy mergers to understand the resulting structures and star formation rates.

Origin of VPOS Dwarf Galaxies

Investigating the formation and alignment of dwarf galaxies in the Vast Polar Structure (VPOS) of the Milky Way.

Timing Argument for the Local Group Mass

Analyzing the rotation curve of the Milky Way to deduce the distribution and properties of dark matter in the galaxy.

Dark Matter from the MW Rotation Curve

Analyzing the rotation curve of the Milky Way to deduce the distribution and properties of dark matter in the galaxy.

Orbits of MW Dwarfs and Globular Clusters

Mapping and modeling the orbits of dwarf galaxies and globular clusters within the Milky Way to understand their origins and interactions.

Parametric Study of Stars with Circumgalactic Disk

Exploring the properties of stars interacting with the circumgalactic medium and its role in star formation and evolution.
