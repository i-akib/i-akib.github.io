Orbits of the Eccentric MW Dwarf Galaxies and High Energy Globular Clusters

We analysed orbits of the eccentric MW dwarfs and high energy globular clusters using their Gaia DR3 proper motion under various MW mass models. With Elodie Dufaux during my M1 project, I identified some close encounters as well as potential groups/structures apart from the VPOS. During my PhD, I supervised Marceau Henry who worked the close encounter of Segue II and Coma Berenices with Sagittarius dsph to identify if Sagittarius affected the orbits and velocity dispersion of these dwarf galaxies.

Collaborators: Elodie DuFaux, Marceau Henry, Dr. Francois Hammer, Dr. Yanbin Yang

Polarimetric Study of Stars with Planets and Circumgalactic Disk

As part of my bachelors research and beyond, I performed data reduction and analysis using AstroImageJ and Astropy for the polarimetric observations of ∼50 stars in the Kepler field with planets and/or circumstellar disks from RTT150 telescope with WeDoWo Polarimeter. We investigated correlation between the polarisation degree of target stars and interstellar medium (ISM), planetary & stellar parameters and polarimetric variation over time. We found excess IR-band polarisation for the KIC stars and very high IR polarisation for Kepler-444. Our findings were presented at the EAS 2022 Annual Meeting

Collaborators: Dr. Agnieszka Słowikowska, Dr. Pawel Zielinski, Gautam Paul
