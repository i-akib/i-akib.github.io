About Me

I use cutting-edge telescopes and simulations to explore phenomena like galaxy formation, dynamics, dark matter and a bit of cosmology in the context of the Local Group that consists of our own Milky Way Galaxy, our closest big neighbour Andromeda and a bunch of small galaxies we call dwarfs.
