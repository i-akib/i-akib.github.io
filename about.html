About Me

I am a doctoral candidate in the Galaxies and Stars pole of LIRA (Laboratoire d'Instrumentation et de Recherche en Astrophysique) at the Paris Observatory - Paris Sciences and Letters (PSL) University. My supervisors are Dr. Francois Hammer and Dr. Yanbin Yang. My PhD with them focuses on the evaluation of dynamical mass of the Local Group galaxies. I was also working with them on the timing argument and VPOS dwarf origin during my masters in astrophysics at the same place.
